Spirit FC Summer Squads

Introduction

The Spirit FC Summer Squads program is designed to provide an intensive training experience for players looking to enhance their skills during the off-season. With expert coaching and a focus on skill development, this program is perfect for dedicated players.

Program Details

The program runs from November to February, with sessions held twice a week. Each session is tailored to focus on various aspects of the game, including ball control, tactical awareness, and fitness conditioning.

Benefits of Joining

Participants in the Spirit FC Summer Squads will benefit from:

  • Professional coaching by certified trainers
  • Comprehensive skill development sessions
  • Exposure to competitive game scenarios
  • Improved fitness and conditioning
